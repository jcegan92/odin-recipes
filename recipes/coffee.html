Simple Coffee

Description

The easiest iced coffee you will ever make

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee
  • Water
  • Ice
  • Creamer
  • Oat Milk

Steps

  1. Put 1 tablespoon of instant coffee into wine glass
  2. Fill with about 1/5 of the cup with water
  3. Add 5-6 ice cubes
  4. Add creamer
  5. Fill the Rest with oat milk
